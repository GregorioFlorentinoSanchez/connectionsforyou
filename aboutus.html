About Us

Founders: Gregorio Florentino and Brandon Knights

We are students looking for opportunities

Throughout our highschool careers, we have seen how our fellow classmates have grown into young adulthood and seeked greater challenges than those offered in school. As the next generation to be in control and with more important choices regarding the future of our country and planet, we must be prepared to face these challenges. Through Connections 4 You, these future leaders and inventors can find their calling and work towards reaching their goals with the support of businessed both large and small.

We seek to help young adults find their vocation, no matter how small. Together, we can make a better world for all.

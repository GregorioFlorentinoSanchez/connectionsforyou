Introducing the website to find internships for exceptional highschoolers.

Connections 4 You provides highschoolers looking to gain experience in a field of their interest with a wide range of choices while being matched with a supportive company who specializes in that trade

Connect students to companies

Students choose what profession they wish to explore and are then matched with companies providing positions

Free and accesible to all

Anyone can download the app and use its easy to understand interface to start their search and, for a low premium, receive additional assistance in their searches

Wide range of choices

Companies in all kinds of industries would be admitted while advisors would assist students in finding the best choice for their interests

Support small companies

Our business would help small businesses or startups who do not have the funds to hire people

Student

Students have the opportunity to search through our extensive portfolio of opportunities and receive assistance from our advisors when making resumes.

Business

Businesses have a place to post their job offers that cater specially to highschoolers and gain visibility among the youth.
